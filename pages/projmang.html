PROJECT MANAGEMENT

My journey in project management began with developing video games at the Game Dev Club at San Jose State University. Many teams in the club had difficulty setting realistic scopes, meeting deadlines, and communicating with each other. Seeing this as a major problem for the club and for myself, I set out to learn the best ways to manage a team to finish a game on time and share it. Since then, I have taken on multiple producer roles in games and hosted workshops on project management at the Game Dev Club. I am currently looking for guidance and a job as a producer or product manager.
