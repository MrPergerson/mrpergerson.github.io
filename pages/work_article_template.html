WORK

Cole Pergerson is a video game designer who creates interactive fiction in virtual worlds. Many of his games are short and fun experiences but his more recent work has become conceptually deeper and presents questions relevant to modern life. He is interested in exploring questions around labor and authenticity. All his games can be found on his Itch.io page, including The Work We Care About, a game that was put featured in the BFA gallery.
