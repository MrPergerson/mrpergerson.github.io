MR. PERGERSON
GAME DESIGN
THE WORK I CARE ABOUT

Released: 2020
Medium: Video Game
Tools: Unity
Type: Collaborative work

The Work I Care About is an interactive text narrative about the hurdles of having a fulfilling life while working at a job. This is a story where readers can make choices that respond to job dissatisfaction and the lack of space to pursue our passions. Every choice confronts the viewer to bring in their own notions of labor and life fulfillment.
