GAME DESIGN

To me, games are fictional experiences that put us directly in interesting situations. The player has agency to create their own decisions and as a designer, I can craft those decisions to be meaningful. If our decisions in real life can have an impact, what kind of impact can they have in a fictional game? I believe meaningful decisions need to be relevant to the person who is playing, which I why incorporate elements of my personal life and world into my games.
