ABOUT

I am a game designer and digital game developer. Many of my games are about personal experiences and issues that I am passionate about. I draw inspiration from anywhere I can to create my games. As such, traveling, reading, photography, working, and other activities are a core part of my process for designing games. I've published my games on Itch.io and you see my other creative work on my website.

Some of the game projects I worked, I was the producer. I've always been curious about what makes a successful team. In my experience with being a producer, I have learned it takes more than clear communication but confidence to speak and take on commitments. I learned to use SCRUM to help myself organize schedules and for my team to work smarter.

I'm studying Digital Media Art at SJSU and I'm very active in the game development community on campus. I have held multiple workshops and other events to help others develop their own successful games. I'm currently looking for opportunities to show off my work or get guidance from a mentor or job.
